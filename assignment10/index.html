Marie Achkar is a sophomore at Syracuse University studying broadcast and digital journalism at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications. Marie is excited for the next few years at Syracuse, where she plans to further develop and utilize the skills that she is learning from the various activities she is involved in.

Future Aspirations?


After college, Marie plans to live in New York City. Growing up in the Midwest her whole life, Marie wants to explore a new area that is more densely populated. She would love to work at Good Morning America or the Today Show. But she also loves content creation and social media management. Either way, Marie strives to go down a career path that will allow her to learn new skills, meet new people and become a stronger communicator.
